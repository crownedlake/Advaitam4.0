About Us

Advaitam the official techfest of national institiute of technology agartala is an eclectic grandure presented solely by the student of the college itself. Advaitam though young at its heart traces back its origination since 2012. Back then it was MOKSHA the cultural fest of NITA and AYAM the techno management festival. Which was graced by eminent persons like SAURABH TIWARY in the year 2012 then VISHAL DADLANI and SONA MOHAPATRA in 2013 when it was actually blooming. THE ADVAITAM officially came into existence from 2014 with the eloping of both this fest to fight for SAAMYA (equality ) in the northeastern region being its prime aim in addition to many other things. It is a 5 day long technical fest circumscribing a wide range of fields from cultural dance poems and performance to technical setups, modern devices,exciting instruments stretching to mind boggling quizzes and many more. Competeition being its motto so it will obviously include teams and solo participants in each category with exclusive prizes and cash-awards. Roughly the following areas are the ones that are going to be covered

Cultural

  • Singing evemts
  • Band performances
  • Exhibitions
  • Fine arts

Technical

  • Robotic events
  • Management events
  • Case study
  • Advertisement and documentary making
  • Fun events

  • Brain teasers
  • Presentation competition

With an objective to encompass all of the aforesaid things nita plans to bring out advaitam 2017 to attract innumerous hoards from among all of the country to participate , rejoice , enjoy and a chance to win in their own and individual way.
