The Institute

National Institute of Technology Agartala (NIT-A), as the name suggests, is one of the quintessential institutes of learning in our country and is arguably the best NIT in all of North-East India.

The Technocultural fest

Advaitam relishes the status of being the biggest Techno-Cultural Fest in all of North-East India. Previously, Moksha and Aayam were celebrated as the Cultural and Technical Fests of NIT-Agartala. But since the past 3 years, both the fests have been merged and are now celebrated as ADVAITAM. The fourth edition of ADVAITAM, which will unveil in the month of March this year, is all set to reach the unprecedented heights with it's wide-ranging array of events and activities.

Media Coverage

Advaitam is covered by every domain of media; from dailies and magazines to radio and TV channels. Some of our previous years' partners are Tripurainfo, Vanguard, Channel V, Big FM, Doordarshan etc.
